Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a scrumptious Italian dish that's like a savory, layered pasta cake. It's made by stacking flat lasagna noodles with layers of delicious ingredients. Usually, you start with a meaty or vegetarian sauce, like Bolognese or marinara, then add a creamy ricotta or cottage cheese mixture, and sprinkle lots of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top. This layering process is repeated until you have a towering masterpiece!

After that, it's baked in the oven until the noodles are tender and the cheese is all bubbly and golden. When you take a bite, you get a delightful combination of flavors and textures, with the pasta, sauce, cheese, and any other ingredients you choose to add, like spinach or mushrooms. Lasagna is a comforting and hearty dish that's perfect for sharing with family and friends. It's like a delicious pasta puzzle! 🍝🧀😄

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
