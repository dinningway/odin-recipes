Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Rich, thick, and tasty. This creamy pasta dish is an Italian staple and one of my favorite meals to eat growing up!

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Romano cheese
  • Pepper cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce
